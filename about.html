Building Brands That Connect

I've spent the last 7 years turning social media into revenue drivers for D2C brands, food tech startups, and SaaS companies. What started as managing a friend's Instagram account has evolved into a data-driven approach that consistently delivers 3x growth and measurable ROI.

I've worked with over 40 brands across beauty, food tech, edtech, and B2B SaaS. My specialty is finding the intersection between creative storytelling and conversion optimization—because great content means nothing if it doesn't drive business results.
